Programs and services

With CNIB, you can achieve seemingly endless opportunities and gain the knowledge, abilities, and self-assurance to overcome obstacles and live the life of your choosing. At CNIB, there's always something going on! Our cutting-edge programs—which range from kid and youth groups to job workshops, leisure pursuits, peer support groups, and tech training sessions—are made to inform, involve, and uplift our participants in all facets of their lives.Canadians who are blind or partially sighted, as well as their friends, family, and caregivers, can access free CNIB programs. Look through our online calendar to find out what's going on in your neighborhood.Our programs are made to meet the requirements of individuals in five different areas, regardless of age:

Live

"Live" programs are dedicated to enhancing daily living skills, achieving personal goals, and fostering confidence in seizing the future. Through tailored support and services, these programs empower individuals to navigate life's challenges with assurance and determination. Whether through education, career development, or social integration, "Live" programs provide the necessary tools and resources for individuals to thrive and pursue their aspirations effectively.

Tech

Our "Technology" programs are designed to unleash the transformative potential of technology, enabling individuals to lead independent lives. By leveling the playing field, these programs offer skills and training that prioritize accessible, available, and affordable technology solutions. Through tailored support and resources, participants gain the knowledge and confidence to harness technology effectively, empowering them to overcome barriers and achieve greater autonomy in their daily lives.

Learn

CNIB's "Learn" programs are dedicated to empowering individuals through education, skills training, and literacy development. By fostering social skills and independence, these programs aim to equip participants with the tools and knowledge necessary to achieve their goals. Through tailored support and interactive learning experiences, CNIB's "Learn" initiatives enable individuals to build confidence, enhance their abilities, and navigate the world with greater autonomy.

Work

CNIB's "Work" programs are designed to equip individuals with the skills and resources needed to pursue their career ambitions, excel academically at every level, and cultivate their potential as entrepreneurs or mentors. These programs offer tailored support and guidance to help participants navigate the complexities of the professional world, whether through skills training, academic enrichment, or entrepreneurial development. By fostering a supportive environment and providing valuable resources, CNIB's "Work" initiatives empower individuals to achieve success in their chosen fields and make meaningful contributions to their communities.

Play

"Play" programs cater to a diverse range of interests, whether it's exploring yoga, discovering the joys of painting, or envisioning adventures at camp. These initiatives are designed to promote an active and healthy lifestyle while providing opportunities for social connection. Through engaging activities and supportive environments, "Play" programs encourage individuals to pursue their passions, develop new skills, and forge meaningful connections with others. Whether it's through group sessions, recreational outings, or creative workshops, participants are empowered to enrich their lives and cultivate a sense of joy and fulfillment.
