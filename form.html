Donate to make Guide dogs accessible to everyone in need !

Give the gift of freedom with CNIB Guide Dogs.
When you donate to CNIB Guide Dogs, you're joining an extraordinary group of people dedicated to changing the lives of Canadians with sight loss.
The cost of owning a guide dog should never be a barrier to independence and your gift ensures the handler never pays a cent.
Donations to CNIB Guide Dogs support the entire program rather than one individual dog, to maximize the impact of your gift.
Why give monthly?
Restrictions caused by COVID-19 have contributed to a surge in demand for guide dogs. Since the pandemic began, applications for CNIB Guide Dogs have increased by 400%. Monthly donations provide critical long-term support that will allow us to ensure no one ever has to wait to access the life-changing gifts of freedom, independence and mobility.
