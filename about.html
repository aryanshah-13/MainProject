About Us

Established in 1918, the CNIB Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to redefining the experience of blindness. Through creative initiatives and effective advocacy, we empower individuals affected by blindness to pursue their aspirations and dismantle obstacles to inclusivity. Our efforts as a foundation for the blind are fueled by a widespread network of volunteers, contributors, and collaborators spanning the entire country.
